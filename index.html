The problem

You read for hours.
Nothing sticks.

Nigerian university students are not lazy. The content they are given just does not help them understand.

"I read but I don't understand"

Textbooks use complex language that takes longer to decode than it does to actually learn the concept.

"My lecturer explains too fast"

A one-hour lecture covers 3 weeks of material. By the time you blink, you have lost the thread.

"Handouts are confusing"

Photocopied notes, unclear diagrams, missing context. The material exists, but it does not explain itself.

"I cram the night before"

Cramming gives a pass. Understanding gives a First Class. Most students never had the tools to do the latter.
